How AquaBridge Works

Farmer Onboarding

Farmers register through AquaBridge to access direct buyer networks and real-time price data.

Product Listing

Farmers update their shrimp or fish availability. Buyers can see live inventory and quality data.

Order & Logistics

Buyers place orders. AquaBridge helps with fair pricing, logistics, and traceability.

Transparent Payments

Payments are made securely and transparently. Farmers receive better rates without middlemen.

Growth & Support

Farmers access expert advice, market insights, and digital tools for better decision-making.
